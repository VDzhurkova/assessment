Our Staff

Only 1 in 10 candidates are successful and offered a job by HighLine. Newly recruited staff go through an initial 1-month probation period, where HighLine monitors their performance and they undergo a series of training days.

Our candidates undergo as follow: Telephone interview – We discuss the applicant’s cleaning experience in detail, and also assess their English language ability, which must be near fluency, to enable easy communication with clients.Face-to-Face interview – We discuss our requirements. We get a better idea of the applicant’s manner. Being able to trust someone in your home, and feel comfortable with them is an aspect that we take extremely seriously. Training days – We ask every candidate to a 2 to 4-hour test clean after a series of training days. One of our senior cleaning staff visits the property for an in-depth inspection.
